Bool-it!

Supported Platforms

Bool-it! makes it easy to simplify your boolean expressions. Whether you're a beginner with Boolean logic or are more advanced with complex expressions, this application is a must-have. With Bool-it!, you may simplify your expressions to two forms including Sum of Products (SoP) and Product of Sums (PoS), view their truth tables and Karnaugh Maps.
