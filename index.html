Hi, I'm Matt, and I develop privacy-driven mobile applications.

Today's mobile applications can be quite aggressive with trying to get our personal information; with some applications, it feels like functionality can come at the expense of our privacy. Why should we settle for this?

With my applications, privacy is paramount, so trusting them should come easy. I focus on supporting various Apple platforms, including iPhone®, iPad®, Apple Watch®, Mac®, and Apple TV®, as well as their services like iCloud®. In short, if you trust their platform and ecosystem, you can naturally extend that trust to my applications.
