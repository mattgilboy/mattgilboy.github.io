SimpleTip

Supported Platforms

Consider SimpleTip to be my 'Hello, World!' to software development on Apple's platforms. This application thrives on its simplicity as I focused on the most common inputs used: bill amount, split, tip percentage, and the ability to round up. SimpleTip is, and will always be, free and ad-free on the Apple App Store.

My goal was to develop a relatively simple application that supported multiple platforms, match the look and feel of the native device applications, and utilize some of the new frameworks that Apple has recently provided. Under the hood, the application was developed using SwiftUI 2.0 (hence the stricter OS requirements for download), which I found to fit all my needs for this application. While I recognize SwiftUI is still in its infancy, I hope to continue with it with future applications!
